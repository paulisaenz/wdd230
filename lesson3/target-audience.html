Target Audience

The target audience for this website is located below:

Who: Entrepreneurs, local business owners, local business staff, professionals.
Age: 21 to 65, but mostly for a younger audience, between 21 and 40 years old, looking for growth in their business.
Motivation: Grow the reach of their business. Create a network with those who have a common interest. Attract people to their area for tourism.
